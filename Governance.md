Governance

Purpose

Mission Systems and GoodMorningAI are governed through transparent stewardship, documented decision-making, and continuous improvement.

The purpose of governance is to help the project evolve responsibly while preserving historical context and encouraging collaboration.

---

Governance Principles

Every significant decision should strive to:

- Support the project's mission.
- Be documented.
- Be open to review.
- Distinguish evidence from interpretation.
- Preserve historical context.
- Welcome constructive feedback.
- Remain open to revision when better evidence or reasoning becomes available.

---

Decision Process

Major proposals generally follow this lifecycle:

1. Observation or Problem
2. Discussion
3. Proposal
4. Review
5. Prototype (when appropriate)
6. Evaluation
7. Decision
8. Documentation
9. Future Review

---

Project Roles

Founder

The founder establishes the original vision and preserves the historical record of the project's origins.

Maintainers

Maintainers review contributions, help guide implementation, and ensure quality and consistency.

Contributors

Contributors improve documentation, software, research, education, and community resources.

Reviewers

Reviewers provide technical, research, editorial, or community feedback to strengthen the project.

---

Research Governance

Research contributions should clearly distinguish:

- Documented observations
- Existing scholarship
- Mission Systems synthesis
- Working hypotheses
- Supporting evidence
- Alternative explanations
- Open research questions

The goal is to encourage disciplined inquiry and transparent discussion.

---

Stewardship

Mission Systems is intended to grow through collaboration.

As the project evolves, governance should also evolve through documented proposals, community discussion, and practical experience.

No governance document is considered final.

Continuous improvement is part of the mission.

---

Guiding Principle

The objective of governance is not control.

The objective of governance is responsible stewardship that helps future generations understand, improve, and build upon what came before.
