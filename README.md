## Mission

GoodMorningAI is the reference implementation of Mission Systems.

Mission Systems is a proposed interdisciplinary framework for helping people and organizations organize knowledge, learning, governance, stewardship, and technology around meaningful missions.

Repository Zero exists to preserve ideas, evidence, software, history, and learning as one connected system.

## What is Mission Systems?

Mission Systems proposes methods for organizing:

- Missions
- Knowledge
- Research
- Governance
- Learning
- Organizational Memory
- Human-AI Collaboration

Mission Systems is intended to evolve through implementation, critique, evidence, and collaboration.

## Liar's Heir Syndrome Research Initiative

The LHS Research Initiative is one research program within Mission Systems.

It proposes investigating whether documented recurring patterns involving inherited narratives, misinformation, propaganda, institutional secrecy, social conditioning, organizational distortion, and related phenomena may be better understood through an integrated interdisciplinary framework.

Mission Systems does not claim these individual phenomena were previously undiscovered.

Rather, LHS proposes a new way of organizing and investigating observations that have already been documented across multiple disciplines and historical periods.

The framework remains open to:

- critique
- refinement
- replication
- competing explanations
- empirical testing
