Code of Conduct

Our Commitment

Mission Systems and GoodMorningAI are built on the principles of stewardship, curiosity, transparency, and continuous learning.

We are committed to creating a welcoming environment where people from diverse backgrounds, disciplines, and perspectives can collaborate respectfully.

Our Values

We strive to:

- Treat everyone with respect.
- Assume good intent while welcoming constructive feedback.
- Distinguish ideas from individuals.
- Encourage thoughtful discussion and evidence-based reasoning.
- Welcome questions and alternative viewpoints.
- Preserve historical context and intellectual honesty.
- Support a culture of learning and improvement.

Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

- Harassment or intimidation.
- Personal attacks.
- Discrimination or hate speech.
- Deliberate misinformation presented as fact.
- Trolling or disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.

Resolving Disagreements

Mission Systems encourages respectful disagreement.

When disagreements arise, contributors should:

1. Focus on evidence and reasoning.
2. Clearly distinguish observations from interpretations.
3. Listen to alternative perspectives.
4. Document unresolved questions when consensus is not reached.
5. Treat every discussion as an opportunity to improve understanding.

Stewardship

Every contributor shares responsibility for maintaining a healthy, collaborative community.

Our goal is not to win arguments.

Our goal is to improve understanding together.

Our Guiding Principle

Preserve the Past.
Understand the Present.
Improve the Future.
